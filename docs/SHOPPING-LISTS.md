## V1.x — Required (Already have or in CHIP)

These come with the TRNTBL or are already built into the CHIP:
- ✅ CHIP v1.0 (C.H.I.P. SBC)
- ✅ RTL8723BS WiFi module
- ✅ NAND flash + Debian Jessie (pre-flashed)
- ✅ sun4i-codec (on-board audio chip)
- ✅ USB-to-UART cable (CP2102, for serial console)

## How to Use These Lists

1. **Pick your region** (US, UK, FR)
2. **Click the Amazon links** → they search for the components on your local Amazon
3. **Select the first result** that matches the description
4. **Add to cart** → typical shipping: 2-7 days
5. **Follow [HARDWARE.md](HARDWARE.md)** for soldering & installation
